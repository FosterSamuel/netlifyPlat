### Sriracha Mayo Surprise
A savory twist on classic Sriracha mayo.
 - 1 part Sriracha
 - 1 part mayonnaise
 - 1/4 part hoisin sauce
 - 1/2 lime, squeezed
 - Salt to taste

### Hot Honey Mustard
Honey mustard with a spicier kick.
 - 1 part mustard
 - 1 part mayonnaise
 - 1/4 part honey
 - 1/4 part hot sauce
 - 1/4 part distilled white vinegar
 - Salt to taste
